About Me

Hi, thanks very much for visiting my page. I am a web development student and designer based in Atlanta, Georgia currently working in specialty food E-commerce Digital Merchandising. I started building web pages back in the infancy of the world wide web in 1997, designing a page for my high school punk band which got a mention in a national magazine since we were one of the few bands who actually had a website at the time! Over the years, I've built many sites for different businesses I've been involved in, including multiple specialty food retailers, recording studios, and record labels. I am currently a student in the Georgia Tech Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, and am excited to share some of my projects here.

Code Refactoring Project

In this Code Refactoring Project, I was tasked with implementing semantic code elements for a business web site to optimize the user experience for guests using screen readers and provide best in class accessibility. Care was taken to maintain the look and feel of the current site as well as maintaining the proper user experience and interface. Both HTML and CSS elements were refactored and streamlined to make the code as dry and comprehensible.

Turnip Truck Ecommerce Website

Designed and maintained full featured Magento v1 ecommerce site for Atlanta based local food company The Turnip Truck of GA. Design stage included: wireframe, page heirarchy, color scheme, all Images including content images and over 1000 product images. Also designed main product category taxonomy and heirarchy for over 2500 individual SKUs. Maintained site content and operation including Magento back end admin super user from 2015 - 2020.

Mixed by Michael Website

Designed business website for audio mix engineer Mixed by Michael on Wix platform. Features included: multiple pages for different business services, embedded music player with current work examples in differnt genres, embedded instagram social media feed, contact forms, and embedded Paypal payments for mix services.

Two Twelve Recorders Website

Designed business website for Atlanta recording studio Two Twelve Recorders. Features included: multiple pages for different business services, embedded music player with current client work examples, contact forms, calendar displaying open scheduling blocks and embedded Paypal payments for studio time.
