About Me

Hi, thanks very much for visiting my page. I am a web development student and designer based in Atlanta, Georgia currently working in specialty food ecommerce Digital Merchandising. I started building web pages back in 1997, designing a page for a music project I was involved with. Over the years, I've built sites for many businesses I've been involved in, including multiple specialty food retailers, recording studios, and record labels. I am currently a student in the Georgia Tech Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, and am excited to share some of my projects here.
